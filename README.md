Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI
Overview

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance across regions, products, categories, and time.
The dashboard supports management decision-making by tracking KPIs, YoY growth, and revenue variance.

Key Features

Total Revenue, YoY Growth %, AOV, Profit Margin

Monthly Revenue Trend analysis

Region-wise performance & variance

Target vs Actual Revenue comparison

Top & Bottom 10 Products by Revenue

Category-wise Revenue Contribution

Dashboard Pages

Executive Overview – High-level KPIs & trends

Regional Performance – Regional growth & target analysis

Product Performance – Product & category insights
